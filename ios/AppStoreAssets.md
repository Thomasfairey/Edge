### Description
Master the art of influence through daily AI-powered training.

The Edge is the first app that combines the psychological canon of Cialdini, Greene, Voss, Carnegie, Kahneman, and Chase Hughes with an adaptive AI engine that simulates your actual professional world.

**How It Works — 10 Minutes a Day**

1. LEARN — A concise micro-lesson on a specific influence technique, drawn from 35+ concepts across 7 domains
2. SIMULATE — Multi-turn roleplay with AI characters tailored to your professional context. Face sceptical investors, hostile negotiators, political stakeholders, and more
3. DEBRIEF — Forensic performance analysis with scores across 5 dimensions: Technique Application, Tactical Awareness, Frame Control, Emotional Regulation, and Strategic Outcome
4. DEPLOY — A concrete real-world mission to execute within 24 hours, with accountability tracking

**Key Features**
- 12+ realistic character archetypes with distinct personalities and tactics
- Adaptive difficulty that adjusts to your performance
- Spaced repetition for optimal concept retention
- Nuance Ledger tracking behavioural patterns over time
- Real-time coaching during roleplay simulations
- Mission accountability system

**Domains Covered**
- Influence & Persuasion (Cialdini)
- Power Dynamics (Greene)
- Negotiation (Voss)
- Behavioural Psychology (Kahneman)
- Nonverbal Intelligence (Chase Hughes)
- Rapport Engineering (Carnegie)
- Dark Psychology Recognition (Zimbardo)

The Edge treats influence the way Duolingo treats language: daily practice, spaced repetition, progressive difficulty, and real-world application.

## App Review Notes

This app uses AI-generated content via Anthropic's Claude API for educational roleplay simulations. All AI characters are fictional. The app teaches influence and persuasion techniques within an educational framework that emphasises ethical application and defensive recognition.

The app includes content about psychological manipulation techniques (gaslighting, DARVO, etc.) presented exclusively through a recognition and defence lens, as specified in the app's "Dojo Framework" ethical guidelines.

Subscription is managed via StoreKit 2. Free tier provides 3 sessions/week with limited domains. Pro tier unlocks unlimited sessions and all content.
